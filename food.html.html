Taka Taka's

One of Bath’s most signature restaurants! Open till 3am, it’s perfect for after a night out - definitely better than McDonalds. It usually has a queue but it moves quickly and it is definitely worth the wait! Especially the chicken gyros.
Nando's

Who doesn’t love Nandos? With a range of piri-pri spice levels, are you brave enough to try the spiciest? Of course you are, everything in Nando's is absolutely delicious and plus its affordable for students! Don't worry, Nando's has mild sauces to offer too!

Wagamamas

If you want a change of pace from takeouts and junk, Wagamama is the place for you! It truly is Japanese cuisine at its finest.
