Hello and welcome to the ultimate Bath Fresher's guide. Congratulations on being accepted to the University of Bath! Now it is time to master your new life here! This website will provide you a series of reccomendations for unforgettable nights out with your friends, activities for weekends, places to go crazy with your bank account and the best places to eat!
There is so much to do in Bath! Our personal favourites for going out include the night clubs OPA! and Zero Zero. For food after a night out forget McDonalds! The queue is going to be insane. Try Taka Taka's instead! You won't regret it. Going shopping on a Saturday? Well you are in luck, Bath has tonnes of shops to offer (even an Urban Outfitters...who would have guessed?). There is so much to see and visit...so go ahead! Look through our different pages and make any requests via the contact form.
